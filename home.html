How Priority Works

A simple 3-step process that saves lives

1

Patient Arrives

Staff registers the patient with basic details, age, and current condition severity.

2

Auto Priority

The system calculates a priority score — elders (65+) and critical patients are automatically elevated.

3

Queue Reorders

The live queue updates in real-time so doctors attend the highest-priority patients first.
